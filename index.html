Who We Are

Nadhari Care Solutions is a forward-thinking healthcare company dedicated to redefining healthcare delivery in Kenya and across the wider African region. Our extensive range of services, including providing high-quality medical non-consumables, personalized home care, and expert psychological counselling, positions us as a key player in the healthcare landscape. With a clear vision to be the most trusted and innovative healthcare partner, we are committed to enhancing the quality of life for our clients by providing solutions that are not only reliable but also tailored to the unique needs of each individual. Our holistic approach to healthcare ensures that we address the physical, emotional, and psychological aspects of well-being, delivering comprehensive care that meets the highest international standards.

Environmental Sustainability

Nadhari Care Solutions Limited is committed to protecting the environment and promoting sustainable practices in all aspects of our business. Our sustainability initiatives include:
